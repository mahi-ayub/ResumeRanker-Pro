Senior Machine Learning Engineer

About the Company:
We are an AI-first company building intelligent automation solutions for enterprise customers.
Our ML platform powers real-time predictions across healthcare, finance, and logistics.

About the Role:
We're hiring a Senior ML Engineer to design, train, and deploy production ML models
that serve millions of predictions daily with sub-100ms latency.

Requirements:
- 4+ years of experience in machine learning engineering
- Strong proficiency in Python and PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience with NLP (transformers, BERT, GPT, sentence-transformers)
- Solid understanding of ML fundamentals: regression, classification, clustering, deep learning
- Experience with model serving (TorchServe, TensorFlow Serving, Triton)
- Familiarity with MLOps: experiment tracking (MLflow, W&B), model versioning
- Experience with data pipelines (Spark, Airflow, or similar)
- Strong software engineering skills: testing, CI/CD, code review
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI, or Azure ML)

Nice to Have:
- PhD or Master's in Computer Science, ML, or related field
- Experience with LLMs and prompt engineering
- Knowledge of computer vision (OpenCV, YOLO, segmentation)
- Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Published research papers or patents
- Experience with Kubernetes for ML workloads

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement ML models from research to production
- Build scalable training and inference pipelines
- Collaborate with data scientists on model architecture and feature engineering
- Optimize model performance, latency, and resource utilization
- Set up monitoring and alerting for model drift and degradation
- Mentor team members and contribute to ML best practices
