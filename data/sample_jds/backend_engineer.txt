Senior Backend Engineer

About the Company:
We are a fast-growing fintech startup building the next generation of payment infrastructure.
Our platform processes millions of transactions daily and serves clients across 20+ countries.

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team and help build
scalable, high-performance distributed systems that power our core payment processing engine.

Requirements:
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and/or Go
- Experience with Django, Flask, or FastAPI
- Deep knowledge of PostgreSQL and Redis
- Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines
- Familiarity with microservices architecture and event-driven design
- Strong understanding of REST API design and implementation
- Experience with distributed systems and high-availability architectures
- Solid understanding of security best practices (OAuth, JWT, encryption)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with Apache Kafka or RabbitMQ for message queuing
- Knowledge of Terraform and infrastructure as code
- AWS or GCP cloud certifications
- Experience with GraphQL
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with gRPC and protocol buffers

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement backend services handling millions of requests per day
- Optimize database performance, query efficiency, and data modeling
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment
- Mentor junior engineers, conduct code reviews, and drive engineering best practices
- Collaborate with frontend, DevOps, and product teams to deliver features
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response
- Contribute to system architecture decisions and technical roadmap

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Remote-first culture
- Health insurance and 401(k)
- Annual learning budget
- Conference attendance sponsorship
